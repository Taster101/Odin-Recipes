Pizza

Chesse Alfredo sauce isnt just for pasta.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cloves garlic
  2. 1/2 tablespoon unsalted butter
  3. Pinch red pepper flakes, plus more for sprinkling
  4. 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  5. 1 1/4 ounces grated Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup)
  6. Kosher salt
  7. Freshly ground black pepper

    8. Steps

    1. Arrange rack in the middle of the oven and heat the oven to 450Â°F. If the dough is cold, let sit at room temperature at least 2 hours before proceeding. The dough is ready when it does not bounce back when stretched.
    2. Mince 2 cloves garlic. Finely grate 1 1/4 ounces Parmesan cheese (about 1/2 cup). Melt 1/2 tablespoon unsalted butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and a pinch of red pepper flakes and cook, stirring a few times, until fragrant and lightly browned, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
    3. Add 1 1/2 cups heavy cream and whisk until combined. Bring to a simmer and cook until the sauce is reduced by half and thick enough to coat the back of a spoon, 7 to 12 minutes. Add the Parmesan and whisk to combine. Taste and season with kosher salt and black pepper as needed. (You should have 1 cup sauce). Remove from the heat and let cool slightly while preparing the toppings and dough.
